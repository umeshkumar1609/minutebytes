Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2024

1. Our Commitment to Privacy

At **MinuteBytes**, we believe your creative work belongs entirely to you. Unlike many online editing platforms, our tools are built on a **privacy-first architecture** designed to keep your files secure.

All image processing across our tools - including **Image Compressor, Image Resizer, Image Cropper, and Image Converter** - takes place **directly inside your web browser**. Your images are never uploaded to our servers or stored by us.

2. In-Browser Image Processing

When you upload an image to MinuteBytes:

  • The image is processed locally within your browser.
  • The image is **never transmitted to external servers** for processing.
  • All editing operations are performed using your device's processing power through technologies such as the **HTML5 Canvas API**.

To enable editing workflows between pages, we may temporarily store image data in your browser using **session storage**. This data:

  • Exists only within your active browser tab
  • Is automatically removed when the tab is closed
  • Is never transmitted to our servers

3. Cookies and Analytics

MinuteBytes does **not use invasive tracking technologies**.

We may use limited and anonymized analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website and which tools are most useful. This information helps us improve the platform but **cannot identify individual users** and is never connected to the images you process.

We do **not sell, rent, or share personal data** with advertisers.

4. Third-Party Resources

Our website may load certain third-party libraries or assets through trusted content delivery networks (CDNs). These may include tools such as:

  • Cropper.js
  • Pica.js
  • UPNG.js
  • Google Fonts

These resources help power our editing tools but **do not receive or process your uploaded images or creative content**.

5. Data Security

Because all image processing happens locally in your browser, your files remain on your device. This architecture significantly reduces the risk of data exposure compared to server-based editing platforms.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect improvements to our services or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us through our website.

Privacy for Browser-Based Image Processing

MinuteBytes is built around a privacy-first model for online image editing. When you use image compressor online features, resize image online free settings, crop tools, or conversion options, processing is designed to happen directly in your browser session. This reduces dependency on server-side file handling and keeps sensitive creative assets closer to your local environment. For users managing product photography, client materials, marketing visuals, or personal media, this approach can lower data exposure risk compared to upload-heavy workflows. The privacy policy explains how temporary session data is used for cross-page editing steps and how long it persists. We keep these details transparent so users understand exactly what happens during tool usage. As MinuteBytes expands, privacy standards remain a core product requirement across new image workflows, ensuring that growth does not compromise user trust or control.
